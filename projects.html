Projects

Managed development team for qinetiq (2022/23, University of Portsmouth): Acted as manager for a team of 8, of both in person and remote members, over the course of and academic year to design a virtual showcase of a vehicle control system with virtual and extended reality capabilities, on behalf of qinetiq,that could potentially be applied to a real vehicle, without looking at what is standard for the military, to instead be creative and make something new. Mainly this involves working on a user interface system for a drone with the quest 2s for the use of VR for complete immersion and XR to see their surroundings and swap between these in a combat scenario.

Developed assets for unreal engine 5 (2023, University of Portsmouth): Worked individually to create modular environmental assets on behalf of my client, Gavin Wade, to be used as part of an upcoming game to be produced in unreal engine 5 as part of my final year project. This gave me the opportunity to research relevant aspects and improvements of the engine such as nanite and how it would impart the production pipeline for game development.
